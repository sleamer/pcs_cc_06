I am a fine artist who also has over ten years of applied artistic retail experience at Trader Joe's. I create and direct the creation of murals, paintings, displays, and sculptures that effectively market a wide range of products while creating a positive shopping experience. Additionally, I have designed and executed commissioned murals and paintings for other retailers and clients. I am very experienced at project management and leading diverse teams to meet and exceed client goals. I bring to all of my work a passion for the intersection of art and science, especially color. I am extremely knowledgeable about optimal ways to utilize pigments, paints, and color attributes to create long-lasting and powerful images, and I have a strong facility for incorporating text and design elements to maximize layout. My ability to translate between the technical and artistic ideas of a variety of stakeholders and audiences is a strength. I also have experience with web development including PHP and mySQL coupled with an in depth knowledge of the Adobe Suite.
